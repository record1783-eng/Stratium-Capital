About Stratium Capital Advisory

Company Overview

Stratium Capital Advisory is an independent advisory and investment structuring firm focused on infrastructure and strategic investment opportunities across Africa.

We support governments, private investors, developers, and institutions in aligning opportunities with commercial and development objectives.

Our role is to bridge the gap between early-stage opportunities and institutional capital through disciplined structuring, coordination, and execution support.

Mission

Our Mission

To bridge sovereign opportunities with institutional capital through structured, execution-focused advisory.

Vision

Our Vision
